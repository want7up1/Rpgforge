# Code of Conduct

RPGForge aims to be a practical and respectful open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critique on code, design, documentation, and reproducible behavior.
- Assume contributors may have different backgrounds, languages, and levels of familiarity with the project.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive behavior.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
